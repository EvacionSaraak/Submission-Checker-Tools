Clinician License Validation Tool

What this tool does:

This tool validates clinician license assignments and category consistency by checking:

  • Clinician License Export: Used to confirm the clinician's name, category, and privileges.
  • Clinician License History: Used to verify license status (active/inactive) and facility matching on the date of service.

Upload the Claim XML, clinician list, and license history Excel files. The tool will compare categories, validate facility licenses, and flag inactive clinicians. Results are shown below and can be exported.
